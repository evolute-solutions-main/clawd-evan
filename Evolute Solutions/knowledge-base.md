## TL;DR
Evolute Solutions sells Meta ads + AI follow-up (SMS) + appointment setting for home remodeling/construction companies.

## Core offer
- **Offer:** Meta Ads → leads → AI follow-up systems to text and book appointments.
- **Win condition:** in-person appointments with qualified homeowners (not junk leads).
- **Pricing / collections:** typically ~$1.5k–$2k collected on the first call; backend revenue often brings total to ~$4k–$8k+ (sometimes >$10k). LTV not yet well-measured.
- **ICP:** established remodeling/construction company with budget to invest, good at closing, good communication.

## Acquisition channels (current)
- Primary: cold SMS.
- Scaling: cold callers / appointment setter team.
- Paid ads: ~$100–$200/day; currently around breakeven; goal is to improve.

## Current bottlenecks
- Not enough calls per day.
- Too many unqualified calls.

## Delivery lifecycle (high level)
1) Client signs → onboarding form + onboarding call in Discord.
2) Connect Meta assets / accounts.
3) Launch ads usually within ~1 week.
4) Ongoing: monitor ads, communicate with client, iterate.
